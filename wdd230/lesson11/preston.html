Activities in the Rain

On a cloudy day such as this your first thought might be that you can't go outside but that isn't true. There may be a few things you can't do that you could on sunny day. On a rainy day like today here are a few things that you can do!

- if you can stand the mess, let them go find all the puddles. If you dont like a mess, you can buy a rain poncho and that will keep your little one clean. - Making origami boats and letting them float on puddles. A straw can help with wind and you can even make it a competion.
