1st Target Audience

The main target audience will be for 25-40yr olds with kids ages 14 yrs old and younger. This is due to to the suggestions that will be given for activities to do outside with kids. It will be mainly focused toward woman as there are more woman homemakers than men but it will not totally disregard men because if they are the homemaker they might need the ideas more than anyone!

1st Persona

  • Age: 25-40yr
  • Sex: Male or Female
  • Main criteria: Children in household

2nd Target Audience

The second target auidence will be for older grandparents who are taking care of their the kids in the mornings while their kids are at work.

2nd Persona

  • Age: 60-70yr
  • Sex: Male or Female
  • Main criteria: Grandparents living close to their children
