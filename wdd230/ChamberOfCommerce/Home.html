Pace Highlights

Blue Angels

A yearly event that brings folks from all over Florida and the surrounding states. This group of 4 planes from the Naval Air Force Base perform a trick show. Including speed racing, loops, and entertain for 2 hours.

The blue angels started out as a group of pilots who liked risks and hada fun and it slowly turned into a group of the best pilots that do a show every year.

Jackson Oak

This historic landmark in the pace area is where the former President Andrew Jackson, while taking a rest after a long battle sat under the tree. It became a favorite tree of his and he would be seen under it every day while in the area during the war.

The Oak tree also became famous as Jackson while caught off guard once by the red coats, hunkered behind the tree for 2 hours story say and was able to hold off a group of scouts until his back up came. Jackson would go on to win the battle there and then eventually be a pivotal part in beating nd capturing the base at Pace.

Whiting Naval Air Base

This base is a staple and recruiting area for navy air officers. It puts out about 40% of the naval air force pilots for the Navy. Many veterans retire here and it has rich navy history.


Many days you will see high up in the sky huge planes going through the sky. it actually takes 3 years of training to become a full pilot in the navy.
