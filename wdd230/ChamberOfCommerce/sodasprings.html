Off the beaten path activities on a sunny day for kids

You look outside and see sunshine and not a cloud in the sky. All types of activities start running through your head. You my think going to the playground or possibly the pool. Why not let your kids be creative and give them big chalk and draw and obstacle course on the sidewalk.

You can draw places where you have to hop a certain amount of times, or spin in circles or get more advanced with handstands and other things. possibly even something for a bike too.
