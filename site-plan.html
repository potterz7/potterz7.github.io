Overview

Purpose

To help cater and provide an overall wonderful experience to those who are serving in the temploe or visiting for religious purposes. The Temple Inn & Suites helps you to feel the Spirit even when you come back from your temple worship, wedding, or youth trip. We believe you should be able feel that same Spirit you feel at home, church, or temple here in our hotel.

Audience

Those members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints who are visiting the temple will love our free Family history Consultation, the kids will enjoy our pools, and even those on a work trip trying to stay close to House of the Lord can get their free fast reliable WIFI for all their work needs. All those that would like to feel closer to their Heavenly Father even if not a member of our faith, as long as they are willing to abide by certain guidelines.

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto Bold 700

I think for something like this website it should be a clean classic style font, I also want it to be a little bolded as it is the heading.

Paragraph Font: Roboto 300

I don't think changing the font family is necessary from the header to the paragraph but the difference in the bold I think adds enough of a difference.
