Overview

Purpose

This will be a side business tghat just focuses on regulkation sized, customizable cornhole boards. This website is to increase interest in cornhole and provide an easy simple way to buy cornhole boards and bags for themselves or for gifts to others.

Audience

The audience will be first for College kids and tailgating events. I will include some advertising for families as well. This is a protable game board that I believe college kids and tailgaters would use most. The next group would be familes going to the beach or on a picnic, since this is a game for all ages.

Paragraph Font: Roboto 300

I don't think changing the font family is necessary from the header to the paragraph but the difference in the bold I think adds enough of a difference.
