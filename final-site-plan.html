Overview

Purpose

This will be a side business that just focuses on regulation sized, customizable cornhole boards. This website is to increase interest in cornhole and provide an easy simple way to buy cornhole boards and bags for themselves or for gifts to others.

Audience

The audience will be first for College kids and tailgating events. I will include some advertising for families as well. This is a protable game board that I believe college kids and tailgaters would use most. The next group would be familes going to the beach or on a picnic, since this is a game for all ages.
