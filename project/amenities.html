You will never want to leave

Come enjoy yourself in our 50x100ft pool. Whether with your kiddos or by yourself. We have free noodles to play with and toys to sink to the bottom so your kids can have fun for hours. Don't like the pool then come relax in our recently installed jacuzzi. Life guard on duty from 9am-5pm. Pool hours are from 6am-10pm.

Don't leave your furry ones behind

Long trips away can be tough to take when you have a furry friend at home. But no need to leave them behind when you stay at the Temple Inn & Suites. Pets are not only allowed but they are welcomed, with designated potty and play areas. When you let us know you will be arriving with your pet we will make sure they have a bed and toys for them to enjoy theirself as well.

Free High Speed Wifi

Whenther you are staying here on a business trip or taking the entire family to the temple. Our free highspeed wifi will be here to put you at ease after a long rewarding day. You may want to search for your ancestors names to take to the temple (make sure to ask for help from our on property family history consultant). Maybe you just need to be able to do work or need some distractions for the kids, either way our free wifi will be there for you.
